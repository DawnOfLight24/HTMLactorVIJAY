Vijay

ACTOR | POLITICIAN

As of December 19, 2025, Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar—revered by millions as Thalapathy—stands at the most critical juncture of his three-decade career. He is currently navigating a historic transition, moving away from his status as the "King of Kollywood" to emerge as a formidable contender for the leadership of Tamil Nadu through his political party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK).

By blending his cinematic farewell with his political debut, Vijay is utilizing his massive fan base to build a grassroots movement. As 2025 draws to a close, he is no longer just an entertainer; he is a man attempting to convert cultural stardom into a transformative political legacy for Tamil Nadu.
